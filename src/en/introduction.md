## Core Value: The Last Mile of Security Protection

SOSI is a high-strength security gateway that integrates identity authentication and remote access auditing. We address the three most critical challenges in remote operations: "Who can get in?", "What can they access?", and "What did they do inside?"

### Strongest Identity Shield

Supports LDAP and **OIDC SSO** multi-factor authentication, with mandatory site-wide two-factor authentication (2FA) to prevent account compromise.

### Precise Access Navigation

Through an administrator-reviewed authorization mechanism combined with the **Role Permissions System**, users can only connect to the specific devices required for their duties.

### 360° Behavioral Surveillance

All operations are recorded with video, keystroke logging, and **text logging (Typescript Logging)** overlaid with dynamic watermarks, leaving no blind spots for unauthorized activity.

### Supported Protocols

Integrates RDP, SSH, VNC, and **Telnet** remote connection protocols.
